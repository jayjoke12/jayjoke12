A bit about me

I'm a developer who enjoys figuring out how things work and building projects that solve problems. Most of what I work on revolves around Java, Minecraft, and experimenting with ideas that push games a little further than they were originally designed to.

I started programming through Minecraft and have been building projects ever since. What began with behavior packs eventually grew into Java mods, rendering experiments, APIs, accessibility tools, and full-stack web projects. I enjoy working across different areas because every project teaches me something new.

One area I'm especially interested in is accessibility. I've been experimenting with computer vision to let players control Minecraft using hand movements instead of a traditional controller or mouse.
